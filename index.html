PPC Agency FAQs

What does a PPC agency do?

A PPC agency such as Santora Technologies specializes in managing pay-per-click advertising initiatives. Agencies like ours plan, launch, and refine campaigns across platforms including:

  • Google
  • Bing
  • Facebook
  • LinkedIn

The goal of these campaigns is to help both B2B and B2C brands generate new leads and customers. Leading PPC agencies provide end-to-end services such as keyword research, ad development, bid optimization, and performance analysis to ensure campaigns deliver maximum results.

How do PPC management services benefit my business?

PPC management services provide a streamlined, hands-off advertising approach, allowing trained professionals to run your campaigns with little effort required from your team. This ensures ads are optimized for strong returns and directed at the right audience at the right moment. Major advantages include greater visibility, increased traffic, better conversion rates, and smarter budget utilization.

Why choose a PPC advertising agency over in-house management?

Partnering with a PPC agency allows you to access specialists who understand numerous ad platforms in depth. A strong PPC team brings together strategists, platform experts, copywriters, designers, landing page builders, and analytics professionals. Hiring this level of expertise internally is often very costly. Agencies also draw on experience across multiple industries, offering insights and tools that may not be available in-house. Working with a trusted PPC agency like Santora Technologies helps save time, reduce costs, and lets your team focus on core business priorities.

What should I look for in a PPC agency?

When selecting a PPC agency, look for demonstrated experience, proven success stories, and open, transparent communication. The agency should provide complete services including market research, strategy planning, campaign setup, continuous optimization, creative design, landing page development, and regular reporting. Ideally, your agency should understand your industry and clearly grasp your business objectives.

How are PPC management services priced?

Pricing for PPC management varies based on campaign size, selected platforms, and service scope. Common models include fixed monthly fees, percentage of ad spend, or revenue-based pricing. At Santora Technologies, we typically charge a setup fee during the initial month for campaign creation, landing pages, and launch. Afterward, we charge a small monthly fee plus a percentage of ad spend, since higher budgets require more work. Fixed monthly plans are also available for predictable spending levels. It’s important to discuss pricing early to find the best structure for your needs.

How quickly can I see results from PPC advertising?

Unlike SEO, which takes time to show impact, PPC advertising can begin delivering traffic and leads almost immediately after launch. However, optimizing campaigns for top performance — such as maximum conversions or reduced acquisition cost — takes time and data. Generally, clearer performance trends emerge after the first two to three months of consistent optimization.

How do I start working with your PPC agency?

Simply reach out to us. We provide complimentary audits of your brand and existing digital campaigns. During a free consultation, our PPC specialists review findings and recommend action steps. If you’re happy with our plan, you can move forward and begin working with our team — and start seeing results driven the Santora Technologies way.

Can a PPC advertising agency guarantee results?

While partnering with a PPC agency like Santora Technologies can greatly increase your chances of success, no reputable agency can promise exact results due to constantly changing market conditions. Factors such as competition, budget, and audience behavior impact outcomes. Honest agencies will set realistic expectations and work consistently to achieve the strongest performance possible.
