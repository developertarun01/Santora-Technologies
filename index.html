PPC Agency FAQs

What does a PPC agency do?

A PPC agency like Santora Technologies specializes in managing pay-per-click advertising campaigns. PPC agencies like ours strategize, implement, and optimize ads across platforms like:

  • Google
  • Bing
  • Facebook
  • LinkedIn

The purpose of these ads is to help their client B2B and B2C brands acquire new leads and potential customers. Great PPC agencies offer a full-service suite encompassing things like keyword research, ad creation, bid management, and analytics review to ensure campaigns reach their full potential.

How do PPC management services benefit my business?

PPC management services offer an efficient, hands-off approach to advertising, allowing experts to handle your campaigns with minimal work needed from your team. This ensures your ads are optimized for the highest possible returns and aimed at targeting the right audience at the right time. Key benefits of PPC management services include increased visibility, higher traffic, and improved conversion rates, all while efficiently managing your advertising budget without stress or effort on your end.

Why choose a PPC advertising agency over in-house management?

Working with a PPC agency gives you the advantage of tapping into a team of experts who specialize in navigating different ad platforms. A good PPC team includes different skillsets including strategists, ad platform experts, copywriters, graphic designers, landing page developers and analytics specialists. Bringing this kind of expertise in-house often comes with a high cost that many businesses can’t afford. PPC agencies also bring a wealth of knowledge from working with diverse clients, offering insights, strategies, and specialized tools that might not be easily available to an in-house team. When you work with a great PPC agency like Santora Technologies, it will save time and resources, allowing businesses like yours to focus on core operations.

What should I look for in a PPC agency?

If you’re hunting for a PPC agency to help your business, you should look for an agency with proven experience – especially for businesses like yours, a track record of successful campaigns, and transparent communication. They should offer comprehensive services, including market analysis, strategy development, full campaign creation and setup, ongoing optimization and management, creative design, landing page development and regular reporting. It’s a good idea to find a PPC advertising agency that aligns with your specific industry needs and has a clear understanding of your business goals.

How are PPC management services priced?

The cost of PPC management services can differ significantly depending on campaign size, chosen platforms, and required service levels. Common payment structures include fixed monthly fees, a share of the ad spend, or a percentage of revenue generated. For example, at Santora Technologies, we initially charge a set monthly fee during the first month for campaign setup, landing page creation, and launching the initial ads. Following that, we implement a low monthly fee plus a percentage of the ad spend, as increased spending often entails additional work on our part. We also have fixed monthly pricing available when the ad spend is within a predictable range. This helps us offer competitive rates that can fit into just about any marketing budget. When hiring a PPC team, always discuss pricing structures upfront to find an agreement that fits your budget and expectations.

How quickly can I see results from PPC advertising?

Unlike organic digital marketing through techniques like SEO (search engine optimization), which take some time to see a benefit, PPC advertising can produce results almost immediately after your campaigns go live. However, optimizing for the best outcomes — like maximum conversions or lower cost per acquisition — can take time, since they require comprehensive research as well as ongoing adjustment based on performance and data. Typically, a clearer picture of campaign effectiveness emerges after the first 2-3 months of a campaign’s life cycle.

How do I start working with your PPC agency?

First, contact us! We offer free complimentary audits of your brand and any existing digital marketing campaigns. During a complimentary consultation, our PPC expert team will share the findings from our audit along with our suggested next steps for improvement. If you like our ideas, then you can sign an agreement to start working with our PPC agency – and can start seeing results the Santora Technologies way.

Can a PPC advertising agency guarantee results?

While working with a PPC agency like Santora Technologies can significantly improve your chances of campaign success, no agency can guarantee specific results due to the variable nature of digital advertising. Factors such as market competition, budget size, and audience behavior all influence campaign performance. Trustworthy agencies – and we like to consider ourselves one of those – will set realistic expectations and work diligently to achieve the best possible outcomes.
